### What is I²C?

Inter-Integrated Circuit (I²C) is a synchronous serial communication protocol developed for communication between integrated circuits using two wires:

* SDA (Serial Data)
* SCL (Serial Clock)

It supports:

* Multi-master
* Multi-slave
* Address-based communication
* Half-duplex transfer

Commonly used with:

* EEPROMs
* Sensors
* RTCs
* Displays
* ADC/DAC devices

f_{SCL}=\frac{1}{T_{LOW}+T_{HIGH}}

t_r \approx 0.8473RC
